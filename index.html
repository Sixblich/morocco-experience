📚 Learn about Morocco

Fascinating facts about the Kingdom of Morocco

🏛️ Capital: Rabat
🌆 Largest City: Casablanca
🗣️ Arabic & Amazigh
💰 Moroccan Dirham
🌍 Africa
👑 Constitutional Monarchy

🏙️ Iconic Places

🌅

Sahara Desert

The world's largest hot desert stretches across southern Morocco with magnificent golden dunes reaching up to 180 meters high.

⭐ Must See
🕌

Marrakech

Known as the "Red City," Marrakech dazzles with its vibrant souks, the famous Jemaa el-Fnaa square, and stunning palaces.

🌟 Most Visited
💙

Chefchaouen

The magical "Blue Pearl" — a mountain town where every street, wall and staircase is painted in breathtaking shades of blue.

📸 Most Instagrammed
🕋

Hassan II Mosque

One of the largest mosques in the world, located in Casablanca. Its minaret stands 210 meters tall — the tallest religious structure on Earth.

🌍 World Record

🍽️ Moroccan Cuisine

🫕

Tagine

A slow-cooked stew made in a conical clay pot. Packed with spices, vegetables, and meat or chicken.

🍚

Couscous

Morocco's national dish — steamed semolina served with vegetables and meat, traditionally eaten on Fridays.

🫖

Mint Tea

The famous "Moroccan whiskey" — sweet green tea with fresh mint leaves, poured from a height to create foam.

🥐

Pastilla

A unique sweet-savory pie made of thin pastry filled with pigeon or chicken, almonds, eggs, and cinnamon.

🎨 Culture & Traditions

🎭
Henna Art

Intricate temporary body art used in celebrations and weddings

🪔
Moroccan Lanterns

Handcrafted metal and glass lanterns that fill streets with warm, colorful light

🎶
Gnawa Music

Ancient spiritual music recognized by UNESCO as Intangible Cultural Heritage

🛒
Souks (Markets)

Labyrinthine markets bursting with spices, textiles, ceramics, and leather goods
